Our Tea Products

Robust & Malty

Black Tea

Step into the world of Indonesian black tea, where robust character meets delightful complexity. Our premium black teas are meticulously hand-picked from the fertile, volcanic soils of West Java, a region renowned for its ideal tea-growing conditions.

We honor the centuries-old traditions of tea processing, carefully oxidizing the leaves to unlock their full-bodied potential. This results in a brew that boasts a rich, often malty flavor profile with a captivating aroma, perfect for a bold morning start or a comforting afternoon escape.

Fresh & Revitalizing

Green Tea

Experience the refreshing and delicate essence of our green teas, a true testament to West Java's natural bounty. Sourced from the pristine, high-altitude plantations nestled in the lush landscapes of West Java, these teas are minimally processed to preserve their inherent freshness and vibrant qualities.

Unlike black tea, our green tea undergoes gentle heating right after harvesting. This careful process halts oxidation, locking in its natural antioxidants, vivid color, and a wonderfully clean taste. The result is a crisp, and incredibly healthy brew that's perfect for a moment of calm reflection or a revitalizing pick-me-up.

Pure & Unblended

Loose Leaf Tea

We celebrate loose leaf tea as the ultimate way to experience tea. Unlike the crushed leaves found in pre-packaged tea bags, our loose leaf teas are whole and unprocessed. This means every leaf retains its full flavor, rich aroma, and inherent health benefits, delivering a superior cup every time.

Curious about how to brew it? It's simple! You can easily steep our loose leaf teas using a variety of methods: a steeping ball, an infuser, a French press, or a strainer. This approach also offers a great benefit: significantly less waste compared to traditional tea bags. Explore our diverse range of loose leaf options, including Gunpowder, White Tea, Green Tea, Yellow Tea, Black Tea, and Oolong, and discover the full potential of a truly exceptional tea experience.
