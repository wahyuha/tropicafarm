Frequently Asked Questions

What types of black tea do you offer for wholesale?

We offer various black tea grades including PF (Pekoe Fannings), OP (Orange Pekoe), BOP (Broken Orange Pekoe), BOPF (Broken Orange Pekoe Fannings), and Dust. Each grade is suitable for different applications and preferences.

What types of green tea do you offer for wholesale?

Our green tea selection includes Chun Mee, Fannings, Gun Powder, Pekoe Super, and Sow Mee. Each variant offers unique characteristics and flavors suitable for different market needs.

What is your minimum order quantity and packaging options?

Our minimum order quantity is 1 ton. We offer flexible packaging options with 40kg or 50kg kraft paper bags, depending on the tea variant. Custom packaging solutions are available upon request.

Where are your tea plantations located?

Our tea plantations are located in the highlands of West Java, Indonesia, known for producing high-quality tea leaves.
