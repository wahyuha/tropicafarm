Green Tea Gunpowder

Our Premium Green Tea Gunpowder, hailing from the pristine West Java highlands, is meticulously rolled into small, spherical pellets resembling gunpowder – hence its unique name. This distinct form preserves the tea's freshness and flavor, unfurling elegantly upon brewing to yield a bright, strong, and slightly smoky cup with a refreshingly crisp finish. It's a truly captivating green tea experience.
